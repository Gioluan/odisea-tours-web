01 / The idea

Nine days. Three federations. One question.

Bring your TOCA squad to Spain. Twenty two players train on the same fields used by FC Barcelona, Valencia CF, and the Spanish National Team. While the players are on the pitch, you and your TOCA staff sit alongside the host coach, watch the session unfold, and debrief afterwards.

That is the question this trip answers: how does a top European academy actually coach? Not the highlight reel. The drill, the cue, the correction, the periodisation.

Players come home better. Coaches come home with a notebook full of methodology nobody in the United States is running yet.

Two nights at the Spanish FA headquarters where La Roja prepares. Sessions led by UEFA Pro licensed coaches from the federation pool.

05 / Nine day itinerary

Madrid in. Barcelona out. Three campuses in between.

Sample structure. Final session times depend on host club confirmation 60 days out.

Day 01
Arrival · Madrid
Land Madrid Barajas. Private coach transfer to Las Rozas. Check in at the Spanish FA headquarters residence. Light recovery walk and welcome dinner with your Odisea ground team.
Day 02
Spanish FA · Session 1
Morning training session at the RFEF, led by a UEFA Pro licensed coach. Coach round table for Sky and the TOCA staff. Afternoon: guided tour of the federation campus, museum, and the pitches La Roja uses. Dinner on site.
Day 03
Spanish FA · Session 2
Second session at the RFEF: small sided and tactical work. Coach methodology debrief and Q and A. Afternoon free in central Madrid for the players (orientation walk with guide). Recovery dinner.
Day 04
Transfer · Valencia
Coach transfer to Valencia along the Mediterranean coast. Hotel check in. Late afternoon: light activation session at Valencia CF facilities. Evening: Mestalla stadium tour. Welcome dinner in the Old Town.
Day 05
Valencia CF · Session 1
Full morning training session at Valencia CF facilities, run by official VCF academy coaches. Coach debrief over lunch. Afternoon: friendly fixture vs a Valencia age matched academy side. Match filmed, tagged per player.
Day 06
Valencia CF · Session 2 + Transfer
Second VCF training session (morning). Methodology folder handover. Afternoon: coach transfer to Barcelona. Hotel check in in the Gothic Quarter. Free evening in the city.
Day 07
FC Barcelona · Session 1
Training at FC Barcelona facilities, run by official FCB academy staff. Coach debrief on the FCB methodology and the rondo principles in practice. Afternoon: La Masia academy visit. Optional Gaudí half day for travelling families.
Day 08
FC Barcelona · Session 2 + Fixture
Second FCB training session (morning). Coach round table on positional play and small sided games. Afternoon: friendly fixture vs a Barcelona age matched academy side. Camp Nou stadium tour for the squad after the match.
Day 09
Recovery, Awards, Departure
Recovery walk along the beach at Barceloneta. Squad awards lunch. Sky and the TOCA staff receive the full methodology folder, match film USB, and player evaluation reports. Coach transfer to Barcelona airport.
